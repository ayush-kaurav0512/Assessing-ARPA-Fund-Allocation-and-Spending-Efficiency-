# Assessing-ARPA-Fund-Allocation-and-Spending-Efficiency-

1. The dataset description:​

What is this dataset about?​

   This dataset concerns the Coronavirus State and Local Fiscal Recovery Funds program as part of the American Rescue Plan Act. The dataset details how financial assistance is distributed to state, local, and Tribal governments across Arizona. This dataset details the funding types and amounts invested in the community and city operations.

  How was this dataset created? By who or which organization?
  
   The city of Phoenix collected this dataset, and the funds were collected from ARPA during the 2021 pandemic crisis.

2. Your team's investigative questions:​
   
   We are very interested in looking into two key points for this specific data set. The first point of interest is to understand which investment focus received the most money from all the types explicitly mentioned. The second point of interest would be to see which organizations received the most allocated money from the ARPA program, and how much of that money was spent versus how much was allocated.
